our speciality

Tasty tajine

Tajine: A slow-cooked stew of meat, veggies, and spices, simmered to perfection in a distinctive clay pot. Rich flavors and tender meat make it a quintessential dish of North African cuisine.

Tasty couscous

Couscous: Steamed semolina grains served with a flavorful mix of vegetables, meat, or fish. A symbol of hospitality and tradition in Moroccan culture, it's a cherished dish enjoyed in homes and gatherings.

soup (harira)

Soupe Harira: A hearty Moroccan soup featuring tomatoes, lentils, chickpeas, and aromatic spices. Often enjoyed during Ramadan, it's a comforting and nourishing dish symbolizing community and tradition. make yourself hot.

Drinks

Tea: Fragrant green tea brewed with fresh mint and sugar, served ceremoniously in small glasses. A symbol of hospitality, Moroccan tea is enjoyed throughout the day in homes, cafes, and gatherings. Keep your body warm

Tasty Sweets

Sweets: Delicate pastries and desserts infused with flavors like almonds, honey, and orange blossom water. From baklava to chebakia, these treats are a cherished part of Moroccan hospitality, enjoyed during festive occasions and celebrations.

Healthy Breakfast

Breakfast: A leisurely meal typically consisting of bread, pastries, olives, cheese, and sometimes eggs or yogurt. It's a time for family and friends to gather, enjoying fresh flavors and starting the day with warmth and connection.
